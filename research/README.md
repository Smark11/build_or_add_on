# Research

The underlying research behind the decision site.

| File | Contents |
|------|----------|
| `full-research.json` | The complete research package: the property dossier, schools, live market & listings, addition cost & feasibility, financing/rates/macro, the 1–5 year inventory forecast, household income & taxes, the 10‑year financial model, the four‑expert adversarial review, and the final synthesis. |
| `addition-deepdive.json` | A dedicated, house‑specific deep dive on addition costs for 2 Clover Lane: priced configurations, line‑item breakdowns, recoup, feasibility (R‑40 setbacks, well/septic, the unfinished basement, the attached garage), and the over‑improvement comp ceiling. |
| `reno-options.json` | Cost research for the reconfiguration options — converting the tuck‑under garage, building a new 2‑car garage, finishing the basement, and expanding the kitchen into the unused room. |
| `floor-plans.json` | The architect + structural team's reconstructed floor plan of the home and the per‑option layouts (room programs, dimensions, structural notes, and the rendering briefs behind the concept images). |
| `design-brief.md` | The project brief / spec that scoped the work. |

## Provenance

The research was produced by multi‑agent workflows (parallel web‑research specialists → a financial model → a four‑expert adversarial review panel → synthesis), then audited by a second adversarial QA workflow. Every quantitative claim traces to a source URL recorded inside the JSON.

## Caveats

- These are **researched estimates**, not the family's confirmed numbers — most importantly, the modeled current mortgage rate (~3.25%) is **inferred, not verified**.
- Figures reflect conditions as of **June 2026** and change over time.
- The current owners' personal names have been **redacted**.
- See the site's methodology section and each source link for detail and confidence levels.
